# Business Idea Exploration Workflow

Interview type for stress-testing and developing business ideas into actionable plans.

---

## Domain-Specific Research (Phase 1)

### BLOCKING Research Targets
- **Market size:** TAM/SAM/SOM for the target market
- **Competitors:** Direct and indirect competitors, their positioning
- **Unit economics:** Revenue per customer, cost structure, margins
- **Existing solutions:** What already exists and where the gap is
- **Industry trends:** Market trajectory, regulatory landscape

---

## Domain-Specific Challenge Angles (Phase 2)

- "Is this a real business or a feature of an existing product?"
- "Who exactly is the customer, and how do you reach them?"
- "What's the moat? How do you defend against a well-funded competitor copying this?"
- "What's the revenue model? Walk me through unit economics."
- "Why now? What changed that makes this the right time?"
- "What's the minimum viable version and how much does it cost to build?"

---

## Domain-Specific Questions (Phase 3)

### Business Model
- What's the revenue model (subscription, transaction, freemium)?
- What's the pricing strategy and how does it scale?
- What are the key cost drivers?
- What's the path to profitability?

### Market & Customer
- Who is the ideal first customer?
- What's the customer acquisition strategy?
- What's the expected LTV:CAC ratio?
- How large is the addressable market?

### Team & Execution
- What key hires are needed first?
- What's the 6-month and 12-month roadmap?
- What partnerships are critical?
- What's the go-to-market strategy?

### Risk & Defensibility
- What's the biggest risk to the business?
- How defensible is the position over time?
- What regulatory considerations exist?
- What happens if growth is 50% slower than planned?

---

## Domain-Specific Output Additions

### Business Case Summary

```markdown
## Business Case

**Value Proposition:** [One sentence]
**Target Customer:** [Specific persona]
**Revenue Model:** [How money flows]
**Key Metrics:** [3-5 metrics that define success]
**Competitive Advantage:** [What's defensible]
```

### Risk Assessment

```markdown
## Risk Assessment

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| [Risk 1] | High/Med/Low | High/Med/Low | [Strategy] |
```

### Competitive Landscape

```markdown
## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Positioning | Strength | Weakness | Our Advantage |
|-----------|------------|----------|----------|---------------|
| [Name] | [What they do] | [Best at] | [Weak at] | [Why we win] |
```
