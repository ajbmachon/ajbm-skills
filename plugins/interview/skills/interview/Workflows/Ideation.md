# Ideation Workflow

Interview type that chains BeCreative's divergent exploration with Interview's structured questioning. For brainstorming, creative exploration, and turning wild ideas into actionable plans.

---

## How It Works

This workflow alternates between DIVERGENT (BeCreative) and CONVERGENT (Interview) phases:

```
BeCreative Diverge -> Interview Constrain -> BeCreative Refine -> Interview Verify
```

### Phase Flow

1. **Diverge** (BeCreative): Generate 5+ diverse options using verbalized sampling (p < 0.10 for at least one). Cast the widest net.
2. **Constrain** (Interview): Apply the Interview constraint capture process. Which options survive? What hard constraints emerge?
3. **Refine** (BeCreative): Take surviving options and explore them deeper. Generate variations, combinations, mutations.
4. **Verify** (Interview): Final interview verification — does the selected direction satisfy all constraints? What's missing?

---

## Domain-Specific Research (Phase 1)

### BLOCKING Research Targets
- **Broad context:** Related domains, adjacent solutions, analogous problems
- **Existing work:** What's been tried before? What failed and why?
- **Inspiration sources:** How do other domains solve similar problems?
- **Constraints landscape:** What are the real boundaries vs. assumed ones?

---

## Domain-Specific Challenge Angles (Phase 2)

- "Is this the most interesting version of this idea?"
- "What's the non-obvious approach that nobody would try?"
- "What would this look like if constraints didn't exist? Now — which constraints are real?"
- "What would you combine this with to make it 10x more interesting?"
- "What's the version of this that scares you a little?"

---

## Domain-Specific Questions (Phase 3)

### Expanding Possibility Space
- What adjacent problem could this also solve?
- What would the opposite approach look like?
- What assumption, if removed, would open new options?
- What's the version that a different industry would build?

### Converging with Constraints
- Of the options generated, which excites you most?
- What hard constraints eliminate which options?
- What's the minimum viable version of the best idea?
- What would you need to see to commit to a direction?

---

## BeCreative Integration

### Verbalized Sampling Parameters
- Generate **5+ diverse options** per divergent phase
- At least one must have **p < 0.10** (low probability, high novelty)
- Grade each option: **Novelty** (1-5) | **Feasibility** (1-5) | **Impact** (1-5)
- Include a **TAIL INSIGHT** explaining what the unlikely option reveals

### When to Invoke BeCreative
- **Phase 1 (Diverge):** After research, before any filtering
- **Phase 3 (Refine):** After constraints are captured, exploring within bounds
- Use BeCreative's extended thinking mode for maximum creative range
